Course Mailing List

All students must subscribe to the course mailing list. Discussions on the list are intended to help you make progress on the labs and in discussing/understanding the papers. List discussions are archived, but the archives are private to the class.

Be sure that you do not send emails that share code or otherwise violate the collaboration rules for the labs. When in doubt, email the course staff first.

To subscribe to the list, go to this link: http://piazza.com/unc/spring2017/comp790130. This requires a unc.edu email address.
